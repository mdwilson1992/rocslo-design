ABOUT US
RocSlo Design is a full-service design firm offering ground-up build, full-scale renovation, and comprehensive furnishing and decor services. Working in close collaboration with architects and builders, our highly skilled and organized team oversees every aspect of the design process including the selection of architectural details and finishes, furnishings, fabrics, accessories, and the custom details tailor-made for your unique living space.
Contact us to request an in-person design consultation.
OUR SERVICES
Interior Design
RocSlo Design is a full-service design firm reviewing high-end new build, gut renovation, and comprehensive furnishing and decor projects in both the residential and boutique commercial spaces. Our mission is to approach each project holistically with a commitment to design spaces that are a true reflection of the clients living in them.
We turn houses into homes, and our California-inspired, eclectic design aesthetic is ever-evolving and always inspired. Our firm is involved in all aspects of its projects from start to finish. We collaborate closely with architects and builders from the early conceptual phases all the way through construction, overseeing the selection of materials, fixtures, finishes, and finally the sourcing, procurement, and installation of furnishings and decor. We’ll transform any space, keeping a keen eye on the details that make it functional, beautiful, and truly one-of-a-kind.
We welcome all full-service project inquiries, both local and long-distance. While based in Los Angeles, our team services projects all over the United States, from California to New York, Texas to Montana, and many places in between.

A native Angeleno, Briyana began her career leading designs for hospitality groups in California, spanning restaurants and cafes to concert venues and corporate offices. Her passion for design transitioned to the world of luxury residential where she thoughtfully brings creative and unique ideas and details to each space.
